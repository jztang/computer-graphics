Final Project Proposal

My final project is going to build off of ray tracing to half spaces, specifically to cubes. I will be creating
a Rubik's Cube, including color. My cube will consist of 27 smaller cubes. Just like a real Rubik's Cube,
the faces on my cube will spin (in groups of 9 smaller cubes).

I want the cube to be interactive via mouse, but I am not sure if I will have the time to implement that.
At a minimum, the cube will animate and solve itself.
